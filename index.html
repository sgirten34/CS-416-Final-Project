Introduction

Electric cars have been a growing segment of auto manufacturing and provide an alternative option that can dramatically increase the fuel efficiency versus traditional gasoline or diesel engines. Production of electric vehicles has increased over the past several years, but overall electric vehicles still represent a small proportion of all vehicles on the road. To better understand the market share and fuel efficiency of electric vehicles, an exploration was conducted on a dataset of vehicles from 2017. This presentation contains a sample of 146 vehicles from 2017 and initally explores the distributions of the fuel type and number of cylinders from this sample. The presentation concludes by demonstrating the dramatic improvement in fuel efficiency from electric vehicles over gas and diesel engines.
